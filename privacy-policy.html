Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how our Screen Recorder application ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Our App is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the core screen recording features.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics (features used, crash reports)
  • Technical data necessary for app functionality

1.3 Recorded Content

All screen recordings and screenshots created by the App are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to, collect, or transmit your recorded content to our servers or any third parties.

2. Bright Data SDK Integration

2.1 What is Bright Data SDK?

Our App integrates the Bright Data SDK to provide you with an ad-free experience. When you opt-in to participate in the Bright Data network:

  • Your device may share its unused resources (such as IP address and bandwidth) to help collect publicly available web data
  • Only your IP address is collected - no personal information is accessed or collected
  • Bright Data does not track your personal browsing or activities
  • You can opt-out at any time through the app settings

2.2 Your Choice

Participation in the Bright Data network is completely optional. You will be asked for your consent when you first use the App, and you can:

  • Enable Web Indexing to get the ad-free version
  • Disable Web Indexing at any time in Settings
  • Change your preference whenever you want

2.3 Learn More About Bright Data

For more detailed information about Bright Data's practices:

  • Bright SDK Privacy Policy
  • Bright SDK End User License Agreement (EULA)
  • Bright Data Privacy Policy
  • Bright SDK User FAQ

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To improve and optimize the App's performance
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • To understand how users interact with the App
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All recordings are stored locally on your device
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • We do not transmit your recorded content to external servers
  • You have full control over your recorded files

5. Third-Party Services

Our App may use third-party services for specific functionalities:

  • Bright Data SDK - For providing ad-free experience (optional, requires your consent)
  • Android System Services - For core app functionality

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we collect
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of optional features (like Bright Data SDK)
  • Uninstall the App at any time

8. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is removed from your device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing in-app notifications for significant changes

10. International Data Transfers

If you use the Bright Data SDK feature, your IP address may be processed in different countries. Bright Data ensures appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion of your personal information.

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
